Corporate finance is a subfield of finance that deals with how corporations address funding sources, capital structuring, accounting, and investment decisions. Corporate finance is often concerned with maximizing shareholder value through long- and short-term financial planning and the implementation of various strategies. Corporate finance activities range from capital investment to tax considerations.
